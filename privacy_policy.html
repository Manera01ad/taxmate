Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At TaxMate, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we collect and use your data when you use our automated tax filing service.

1. Data Collection

We collect information provided by you via WhatsApp, including your name, PAN, income details, and contact information, solely for the purpose of tax calculation and filing.

2. Use of Data

Your data is used to generate tax computation sheets and file returns with the Income Tax Department. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Security

We use industry-standard encryption and secure integrations with Google Sheets and WhatsApp Business API to protect your information.

4. Data Retention

We retain your tax records as required by the Indian IT Act 2000. You may request deletion of your conversation data at any time.
